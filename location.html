Finding Our Camp

  • Look for the large crystal formations on our shade structure
  • Our camp flag features a glowing green crystal ship design
  • Listen for our sound system playing ambient electronic music
  • The art car is typically parked at camp when not roaming the playa

Visiting Hours

Our camp is open to visitors daily from 10am to 2am. Outside these hours, please respect our camp members' rest time.

Nearby Landmarks

  • Two blocks from the 7:30 portal
  • Near the "Cosmic Playground" theme camp
  • Look for the large geodesic dome with LED lighting
