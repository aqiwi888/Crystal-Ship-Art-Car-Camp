About Our Camp

The Crystal Ship is a Burning Man art car camp featuring a magnificent vehicle adorned with glowing crystal formations. Our camp provides a unique experience for Burners, offering rides, events, and a welcoming community space.

Join us for daily adventures across the playa, evening light shows, and special events throughout the week. Our camp members are dedicated to creating magical experiences and fostering connections within the Burning Man community.
